Menu

ICB

The ICB is a pizza that is made with a thin crust and is topped with cheese and pepperoni. It is a very popular pizza and is a great choice for anyone who is looking for a quick meal.

Hot-N-Ready Pizza

The Hot-N-Ready is just a classic pepperoni pizza that is ready to pickup at any time during the day. It has a great price at only $5.99 for a whole pizza. It is a great choice for anyone who is looking for a quick pizza.

Crazy Bread

Crazy Bread is a great side to any pizza. It is a breadstick that is covered in garlic butter and parmesan cheese. It is a great choice for anyone who is looking for a great side to their pizza.

Large Cheese Pizza

The Large Cheese Pizza is a great choice for anyone who is looking for a large pizza that is just cheese. Since some poepole do not like toppings on their pizza this would be a perfect choice for them. It is a great tasty choice for a pizza.
